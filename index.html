About Us

We Frame Moments
That Matter

Citra Art Frame was born from a passion for preserving life's most precious moments. Based in Indonesia, we specialize in handcrafted custom photo frames tailored to your exact specifications.

Whether it's a wedding portrait, a family milestone, or a piece of art — we believe every image deserves a frame as unique as the story behind it. We offer a range of materials, glass options, and sizes to fit any need and budget.

Each frame is measured, cut, and assembled with care — no mass production, no shortcuts. Just quality craftsmanship delivered with pride.
